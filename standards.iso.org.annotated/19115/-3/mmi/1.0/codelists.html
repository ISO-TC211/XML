MD_MaintenanceFrequencyCode ( MD_MaintenanceFrequencyCode ):

Description: frequency with which modifications and deletions are made to the data after it is first produced
Entry Definition
MD_MaintenanceFrequencyCode_continual resource is repeatedly and frequently updated
MD_MaintenanceFrequencyCode_daily resource is updated each day
MD_MaintenanceFrequencyCode_weekly resource is updated on a weekly basis
MD_MaintenanceFrequencyCode_fortnightly resource is updated every two weeks
MD_MaintenanceFrequencyCode_monthly resource is updated each month
MD_MaintenanceFrequencyCode_quarterly resource is updated every three months
MD_MaintenanceFrequencyCode_biannually resource is updated twice each year
MD_MaintenanceFrequencyCode_annually resource is updated every year
MD_MaintenanceFrequencyCode_asNeeded resource is updated as deemed necessary
MD_MaintenanceFrequencyCode_irregular resource is updated in intervals that are uneven in duration
MD_MaintenanceFrequencyCode_notPlanned there are no plans to update the data
MD_MaintenanceFrequencyCode_unknown frequency of maintenance for the data is not known
MD_MaintenanceFrequencyCode_periodic resource is updated at regular intervals
MD_MaintenanceFrequencyCode_semimonthly resource updated twice a monthly
MD_MaintenanceFrequencyCode_biennially resource is updated every 2 years
