CI_DateTypeCode ( CI_DateTypeCode ):

Description: identification of when a given event occurred
Entry Definition
CI_DateTypeCode_creation date identifies when the resource was brought into existence
CI_DateTypeCode_publication date identifies when the resource was issued
CI_DateTypeCode_revision date identifies when the resource was examined or re-examined and improved or amended
CI_DateTypeCode_expiry date identifies when resource expires
CI_DateTypeCode_lastUpdate date identifies when resource was last updated
CI_DateTypeCode_lastRevision date identifies when resource was last reviewed
CI_DateTypeCode_nextUpdate date identifies when resource will be next updated
CI_DateTypeCode_unavailable date identifies when resource became not available or obtainable
CI_DateTypeCode_inForce date identifies when resource became in force
CI_DateTypeCode_adopted date identifies when resource was adopted
CI_DateTypeCode_deprecated date identifies when resource was deprecated
CI_DateTypeCode_superseded date identifies when resource was superseded or replaced by another resource
CI_DateTypeCode_validityBegins time at which the data are considered to become valid. NOTE: There could be quite a delay between creation and validity begins
CI_DateTypeCode_validityExpires time at which the data are no longer considered to be valid
CI_DateTypeCode_released the date that the resource shall be released for public access
CI_DateTypeCode_distribution date identifies when an instance of the resource was distributed

CI_PresentationFormCode ( CI_PresentationFormCode ):

Description: mode in which the data are represented
Entry Definition
CI_PresentationFormCode_documentDigital digital representation of a primarily textual item (can contain illustrations also)
CI_PresentationFormCode_documentHardcopy representation of a primarily textual item (can contain illustrations also) on paper, photographic material, or other media
CI_PresentationFormCode_imageDigital likeness of natural or man-made features, objects, and activities acquired through the sensing of visual or any other segment of the electromagnetic spectrum by sensors, such as thermal infrared, and high resolution radar and stored in digital format
CI_PresentationFormCode_imageHardcopy likeness of natural or man-made features, objects, and activities acquired through the sensing of visual or any other segment of the electromagnetic spectrum by sensors, such as thermal infrared, and high resolution radar and reproduced on paper, photographic material, or other media for use directly by the human user
CI_PresentationFormCode_mapDigital map represented in raster or vector form
CI_PresentationFormCode_mapHardcopy map printed on paper, photographic material, or other media for use directly by the human user
CI_PresentationFormCode_modelDigital multi-dimensional digital representation of a feature, process, etc.
CI_PresentationFormCode_modelHardcopy 3-dimensional, physical model
CI_PresentationFormCode_profileDigital vertical cross-section in digital form
CI_PresentationFormCode_profileHardcopy vertical cross-section printed on paper, etc.
CI_PresentationFormCode_tableDigital digital representation of facts or figures systematically displayed, especially in columns
CI_PresentationFormCode_tableHardcopy representation of facts or figures systematically displayed, especially in columns, printed on paper, photographic material, or other media
CI_PresentationFormCode_videoDigital digital video recording
CI_PresentationFormCode_videoHardcopy video recording on film
CI_PresentationFormCode_audioDigital digital audio recording
CI_PresentationFormCode_audioHardcopy audio recording delivered by analog media, such as a magnetic tape
CI_PresentationFormCode_multimediaDigital information representation using simultaneously various digital modes for text, sound, image
CI_PresentationFormCode_multimediaHardcopy information representation using simultaneously various analog modes for text, sound, image
CI_PresentationFormCode_physicalSample a physical object, e.g. Rock or mineral sample, microscope slide
CI_PresentationFormCode_diagramDigital information represented graphically by charts such as pie chart, bar chart, and other type of diagrams and recorded in digital format
CI_PresentationFormCode_diagramHardcopy information represented graphically by charts such as pie chart, bar chart, and other type of diagrams and printed on paper, phototographic material, or other media

CI_RoleCode ( CI_RoleCode ):

Description: function performed by the responsible party
Entry Definition
CI_RoleCode_resourceProvider party that supplies the resource
CI_RoleCode_custodian party that accepts accountability and responsibility for the resource and ensures appropriate care and maintenance of the resource
CI_RoleCode_owner party that owns the resource
CI_RoleCode_user party who uses the resource
CI_RoleCode_distributor party who distributes the resource
CI_RoleCode_originator party who created the resource
CI_RoleCode_pointOfContact party who can be contacted for acquiring knowledge about or acquisition of the resource
CI_RoleCode_principalInvestigator key party responsible for gathering information and conducting research
CI_RoleCode_processor party who has processed the data in a manner such that the resource has been modified
CI_RoleCode_publisher party who published the resource
CI_RoleCode_author party who authored the resource
CI_RoleCode_sponsor party who speaks for the resource
CI_RoleCode_coAuthor party who jointly authors the resource
CI_RoleCode_collaborator party who assists with the generation of the resource other than the principal investigator
CI_RoleCode_editor party who reviewed or modified the resource to improve the content
CI_RoleCode_mediator a class of entity that mediates access to the resource and for whom the resource is intended or useful
CI_RoleCode_rightsHolder party owning or managing rights over the resource
CI_RoleCode_contributor party contributing to the resource
CI_RoleCode_funder party providing monetary support for the resource
CI_RoleCode_stakeholder party who has an interest in the resource or the use of the resource
